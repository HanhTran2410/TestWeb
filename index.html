Our PORTFOLIO

IDC White House - Da Nang


Danang is Vietnam’s third largest city and located in the Central Coast region on the East Sea. Danang beach is one of the World’s top 10 ranking beaches. Danang City is known as the most worthworthwhile city to live in Vietnam. IDC White House Villas and Apartments situated 25 minutes from both Danang International Airport (DAD) and Hoi An Ancient Town - the UNESCO World Heritage Sites. IDCWH is offering to your family The Ocean Villas and The Point Villas, just within easy reach to some of the country’s finest attractions. From scenic beaches, islands to Marble mountain and national parks, 80 local markets to the My Son Sanctuary and Hue Complex of Monuments, guests have ample opportunity to explore, discover and immerse themselves in Vietnam’s unique culture.

IDC White House - Germany


Expanding our portfolio from Vietnam to Germany, we offer apartment buildings for long-term rental in Frankfurt (Oder). Frankfurt an der Oder is famous for its red-brick Gothic buildings, located in the German state of Brandenburg. By the Oder river, it lies directly on the border with Slubice, Poland. The apartments are fully / partially furnished with modern design, central heating in city centers. The famous attractions are the Town Hall, parks and museums.

IDC White House - Ha Long


Located in the most beautiful and unique area of Halong with a spectacular direct view to the World’s Natural Heritage Halong Bay. The suites and villas are located in FLC Halong Bay Golf & Luxury resort, offering clubhouse, bars, indoor and outdoor pools, International Convention center, one of the most beautiful 18-hole Golf courses in Vietnam and many other services.

IDC White House - Ha Noi


IDC White House Hanoi is our Head Quarter, located in the Capital of Vietnam - one of the world’s most ancient capitals. This is the second-biggest city in Vietnam, home of almost 10 millions people. Hanoi is one of the fastest growing cities in SEA with many buildings and projects up-raising. IDC White House offers modern and fully furnished apartments in the top area. As well as retail and offices for rent in the high-rise buildings and newly-developed area.
